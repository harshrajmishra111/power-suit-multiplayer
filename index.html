üéØ Objective

Win tricks equal to your bid and score points. The player with the highest total score wins!

üé¥ Game Setup

  • 4 Players: Use all 52 cards

‚ö° The Power Set (Trump Suit)

The Trump Suit has ultimate power:

  • ANY Trump card beats ANY non-Trump card
  • Even a 2 of Trump beats an Ace of another suit!
  • Cards in the Power Set have a golden glow ‚ú®

Phase 1: Bidding (60 seconds)

Phase 2: Playing Tricks

13 rounds of card play. Follow the rules below to play cards correctly.

üÉè Card Playing Rules (CRITICAL)

These rules MUST be followed in order:

  1. Must Follow Suit: If you have the led suit, you MUST play it
  2. Must Play Higher: If you have a higher card than what's been played, you MUST play higher
  3. Can Play Any Lower: If all your cards are lower, play any of them
  4. Trump Only When Out: Only play Trump when you have NO cards of the led suit
